Duration and Data Bytes

Graph file: cdf_duration_bytes.py
Main fields:

conn.attr[co.DURATION]                     # Duration of the connection seen by mptcptrace
conn.attr[direction][co.BYTES_MPTCPTRACE]  # Number of unique bytes seen by mptcptrace

Additional information

Those fields are output of mptcptrace, but similar information can be obtained via information of connection subflows coming from tstat.
